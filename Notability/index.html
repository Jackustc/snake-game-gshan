FAQ

Quick answers

Is Notability free for students?

Notability offers limited free access and paid plans. Some institutions may provide student discounts or licenses.

Can Notability convert handwriting to text?

Yes. It supports handwriting recognition and OCR so handwritten notes can be searched and converted.

Does Notability support cloud syncing?

Yes. iCloud sync keeps notes available across supported Apple devices.

Can I record lectures while taking notes?

Yes. Audio recordings can sync with notes so you can revisit the corresponding moment later.

Does it work on Android or Windows?

Notability is primarily designed for iPad, iPhone, and Mac rather than native Android or Windows use.
